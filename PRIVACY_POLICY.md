# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

## Overview

Kaze is a private digital hospitality platform developed by GABO for hotel guests, event attendees, day visitors, and related hospitality users.

This Privacy Policy explains how Kaze may collect, use, store, and protect personal data when the product is used in business, pilot, or production environments.

Contact:
- See [CONTACT.md](CONTACT.md)

## Who We Are

Kaze is operated as a private commercial project by GABO.

Depending on the deployment model, personal data may be processed:
- by GABO as the product provider
- by a hotel, venue, or business customer using Kaze
- by both parties under a commercial agreement

Exact controller/processor roles may depend on the final contract and deployment setup.

## Information We May Collect

Depending on the product features enabled, Kaze may process:

- guest identity information
  - name
  - stay identifier
  - room assignment
  - booking-related references
- service request data
  - late checkout requests
  - housekeeping requests
  - concierge requests
  - custom request notes
- itinerary and access data
  - active stay context
  - schedule items
  - event registrations
  - access entitlements
- map/navigation data
  - selected routes
  - destination lookups
  - floor selections
- technical and device data
  - app version
  - device type
  - OS version
  - crash diagnostics
  - notification permission status
- communication and notification data
  - push notification preferences
  - delivery status
- support and business communications
  - emails or messages sent to the Kaze team

## How We Use Information

We may use information to:

- provide digital guest services
- display hotel stay information
- enable map navigation and venue discovery
- process and track service requests
- personalize guest-facing recommendations
- maintain security and access control
- improve product quality and reliability
- diagnose bugs and operational failures
- communicate service-related updates
- comply with legal or contractual obligations

## Legal Bases

Depending on jurisdiction and deployment, processing may rely on:

- performance of a contract
- legitimate interests
- compliance with legal obligations
- consent, where required

## Data Sharing

We may share relevant data with:

- hotels, venues, and hospitality operators using Kaze
- service providers that support hosting, infrastructure, analytics, notifications, or operations
- legal or regulatory authorities when required by law
- contractors or team members working under confidentiality, employment, or paid service agreements

We do not authorize public reuse, resale, or open redistribution of private customer data.

## Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as needed for:

- active hospitality operations
- support and diagnostics
- legal and contractual obligations
- legitimate business record-keeping

Specific retention periods may later be defined in customer agreements or internal retention schedules.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, including:

- access controls
- limited internal access
- secure storage practices
- environment separation where applicable
- ongoing architecture and security improvements

However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## International Processing

Depending on infrastructure and customer setup, data may be processed in countries other than the user’s own. Appropriate contractual and technical measures should be used where required.

## Children

Kaze is not intentionally designed as a general consumer app for children. If child-related data is processed through a hospitality or event context, that processing should be governed by the applicable customer relationship and legal requirements.
