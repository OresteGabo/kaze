# Contribution Policy

Kaze is a private commercial project.

Public contributions are not accepted.

## Who May Contribute

Contributions are restricted to:

- authorized internal team members
- paid contractors
- persons working under a signed employment, contractor, NDA, or other written agreement with GABO

## External Contributions

Unsolicited pull requests, patches, copies, forks for redistribution, or public derivative work proposals are not accepted.

## Why

This project contains private product strategy, business logic, proprietary branding, and commercial implementation details intended for controlled business use only.
