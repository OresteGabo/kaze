\section{Core Vision}

Kaze is evolving from a hospitality app into a broader venue experience platform.

The long-term idea is to combine:
\begin{itemize}
    \item venue maps
    \item access control
    \item reservations
    \item local payments
    \item add-on services
    \item white-label venue experiences
\end{itemize}

\section{Primary Opportunity Areas}

\begin{longtable}{p{0.28\textwidth} p{0.64\textwidth}}
\textbf{Area} & \textbf{Why It Matters} \\
Hotels & strong starting market for guest flows, services, access, and indoor maps \\
Conference venues & high need for room discovery, access rules, reservations, and attendee movement \\
Wedding venues & strong fit for layout planning, event styling, booking deposits, and add-on services \\
Stadiums & fits seating maps, ticket/pass entry, and zone-based access \\
Government buildings & fits indoor guidance and access-aware navigation \\
\end{longtable}
